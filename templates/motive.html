Blood Donation

Today, most blood donors in developed countries are unpaid volunteers who donate blood for community supply. Some countries have established blood banks while others depend on family members to donate blood for their loved ones. Blood donation has several reasons including charity, social awareness, and helping friends or relatives. Despite the many reasons, the number of active donors is insufficient. However, during disasters, blood donations rise significantly, sometimes leading to excess supply.
